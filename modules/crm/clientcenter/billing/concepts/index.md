# Concepts

Use the pages in this section to understand how the **Billing** area in Client Center works.

These pages explain how invoices, due payments, and payment history are presented to external users, how invoice-related files are shown, and how billing information depends on the assigned external role and current customer context.

Unlike the task-oriented guidance in [Operations](../operations/index.md), the pages here focus on the logic and behavior of the Billing section.

## In this section

- [Invoices page](invoices-page.md)  
  Explains how invoice documents are shown in Client Center, what users can see in the invoice list and document view, and how attached files appear in an invoice.

- [Due Payments page](due-payments-page.md)  
  Explains how Client Center presents currently due payment obligations for the selected customer.

- [Payment History page](payment-history-page.md)  
  Explains how completed payment records are shown in Client Center.

## Recommended reading order

If you are new to the Billing section, read the pages in this order:

1. [Invoices page](invoices-page.md)
2. [Due Payments page](due-payments-page.md)
3. [Payment History page](payment-history-page.md)

This sequence starts with invoice documents and then continues with the current and historical payment views related to them.
