# Concepts

Use the pages in this section to understand how the **Customer Profile** area in Client Center works.

These pages explain how customer information is presented, how customer-related files are exposed in the profile, and how the visible content depends on the current customer context and access model.

Unlike the task-oriented guidance in [Operations](../operations/index.md), the pages here focus on the logic and behavior of the Customer Profile section.

## In this section

- [Customer information](customer-information.md)  
  Explains what company information is shown in Customer Profile, including identifiers such as **UIN** and **VAT number**.

- [Files in Customer Profile](files-in-customer-profile.md)  
  Explains how customer-related files are shown in the profile and how their visibility depends on external access and file sharing configuration.

## Recommended reading order

If you are new to the Customer Profile section, read the pages in this order:

1. [Customer information](customer-information.md)
2. [Files in Customer Profile](files-in-customer-profile.md)

This sequence starts with the company information shown in the profile and then continues with the file-related behavior of the page.
