# Troubleshooting

Use the pages in this section to diagnose and resolve the most common problems in **Client Center**.

These pages are organized by symptom. They help identify whether the issue is related to access roles, customer assignment, file visibility, Client Center configuration, or specific order and user-management conditions.

Unlike the conceptual pages in [Concepts](../concepts/index.md), the pages here are problem-oriented and focus on why something is missing, unavailable, or not working as expected.

## In this section

- [Error codes](error-codes.md)  
  Lists the Client Center error codes and explains what each one means.

- [Cannot access a page or section](cannot-access-a-page-or-section.md)  
  Explains why a user may be unable to open part of Client Center and what to check in the access model.

- [New Order is not available](new-order-is-not-available.md)  
  Explains why the **New Order** function may be missing or unavailable.

- [Cannot add a user](cannot-add-a-user.md)  
  Explains the common reasons why an external user cannot be added in User Management.

- [Files are not visible or downloadable](files-are-not-visible-or-downloadable.md)  
  Explains why files may not appear in Client Center or cannot be downloaded.

- [Settings changes are not applied](settings-changes-are-not-applied.md)  
  Explains what to check when Client Center settings have been changed but the expected result is not visible.

## How to use this section

Start with the symptom that best matches the issue you are investigating.

If the issue includes a Client Center error code, first check [Error codes](error-codes.md). Otherwise, use the symptom-based pages to identify the likely cause and the relevant area to verify.

In most cases, problems in Client Center are related to one of the following:

- the user's assigned external role;
- the customer-specific access assignment;
- missing or incorrect Client Center settings;
- file sharing configuration through **Access Permissions**;
- the current customer context.
