## Product Prices

**Product Prices** define the sales prices that can be applied to products under specific commercial conditions.

They are used when a product can have different prices depending on business context, such as the customer, quantity, validity period, price list, or other pricing conditions.

A product can have multiple price records at the same time. During sales processing, @@name evaluates the applicable product prices and selects the one that best matches the current conditions according to the pricing rules.

Product Prices support scenarios such as standard prices, customer-specific prices, quantity-based prices, time-limited prices, and other condition-based pricing rules.
